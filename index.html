Introducing Btapp.js

An easy way to build BitTorrent web apps.

Built on Backbone

Relies on Backbone to make turbulant data a breeze to work with. Leans on Backbone's event triggering to make information such as torrent file download rates easy to track.

Torrents

Now magnet links, torrent urls, or even just info hashes are all you need to download content to your users computer. Brought to you by BitTorrent Inc., the birthplace of the protocol, and home to uTorrent and BitTorrent torrent clients.

More than just downloads

Stream while the torrent download is in progress, move files to iTunes, interact with the underlying DHT, and more!
